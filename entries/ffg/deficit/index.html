In 2018, the federal government spent $779 billion more than it collected, resulting in a deficit.

779 dots x $1 billion = $779 billion

How did we end up with a deficit? A deficit occurs when the money going out exceeds the money coming in. Since the federal government spent $4.1 trillion and collected $3.3 trillion in 2018, the government ended up with a deficit for the year.
